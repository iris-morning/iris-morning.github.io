这三十年

A Journey of Thirty Years: Every step leads to a better self.

1997 · 始于南疆

1997年，我在南疆出生。那时候的冬天很冷，妈妈爸爸辛苦啦。

Born in Southern Xinjiang, 1997. The winters back then were biting cold. Thank you, Mom and Dad, for everything.

2001 · “一分钟”约定

上幼儿园了，分离焦虑让我每天嚎哭。我时常伸出食指对着母亲：意思是，你一分钟以后就要来接我。

Starting kindergarten with terrible separation anxiety. I’d cry every day, holding up a finger to my mom—a silent plea for her to come back for me in just "one minute."

2003 · 满分卷与木屑香

小学第一次期末考了100分。父亲在做木工，身边满是木屑，我骄傲地向他展示我的成绩。

My first perfect score in elementary school. I remember the smell of fresh wood shavings as my father worked on his carpentry, and the pride I felt showing him that 100.

2009 · 尘土飞扬的青春

初中的太阳很晒，学校里到处是土路，环形跑道很破旧。那是汗水与尘土交织的青春。

Junior high was marked by a scorching sun and dusty dirt tracks. The school was worn down, but it was there that my youth began to unfold amidst sweat and dust.

2012 · 炸串与物理题

在市里念高中。记得校门口香气扑鼻的炸串，也记得那张总也考不及格、让人头疼的物理试卷。

High school in the city. The nostalgia of sizzling street food at the school gate mixed with the headache of physics exams I could never quite pass.

2015 · 梦里的江南

去南方水乡念大学。满眼郁郁葱葱，空气湿润，建筑温婉。这里的一切对我来说都是新鲜的。

Heading south for college. A world of lush greenery, humid air, and elegant architecture. Everything felt so incredibly fresh and full of wonder.

2019 · 孤独的攀登者

2019年，在乌鲁木齐工作。面对看不完的图纸和表格，在厚雪覆盖的寒冬，我经常买打折蔬菜，一个人咬牙坚持考下了一级造价工程师证。

Back in Urumqi for work. Endless blueprints and spreadsheets under a blanket of heavy winter snow. Living on discounted groceries, I pushed through the solitude to earn my Class 1 Cost Engineer certification.

2022 · 杭州的AB面

杭州滋养了我，有西湖徒步的欢愉，也有高压加班和独自做肠胃镜时难以忘记的孤独。

Hangzhou nourished my soul. I found joy in hiking by the West Lake with friends, yet I also endured the crushing weight of overtime and the unforgettable loneliness of undergoing a gastroscopy all by myself.
